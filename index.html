Jawaharlal Nehru Sports Trust

Promoting Sports through Tournaments, Student Exchange Programmes, International Tourneys, Sports Infrastructure, Charity Events, globally, by Supporting Sports Bodies, Organizations, Schools, Corporations, NGO’s and others, in consequence, empowering the youth, both boys and girls, since 1984





Explore More

VISION

Inclusion, Upliftment and Empowerment of youth, both Boys and Girls, through the medium of Sports, Globally.

MISSION

  1. To help Sports bodies / organizations with initiatives in planning and executing global Sports tournaments and tours.
  2. To assist Corporates in developing and organizing youth centric Sports and Cultural programmes, as part of their Corporate Social Responsibility ( CSR ).
  3. To support School / Colleges in development of their Sports Programme / Calendar as also in Organizing cross border Multi-Sport and Cultural Exchange programmes for them.
  4. To help State and Federal Governments in the development of Sports Infrastructure for the development and empowerment of Youth.
  5. To help other similar non-profit bodies, regional and global, with rural development and upliftment programmes directed for the youth.
  6. To help all other individuals / groups working for the youth through the medium of Sports.
